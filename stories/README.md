# Stories

Welcome to the **Stories** section of *Founder in the Wild*.  

This is where real founders share their experiences attending live events to represent their startups: the wins, the mistakes, and the lessons learned. These stories are the heart of the playbook: **honest, practical, and actionable**.

---

## Purpose

- Capture real experiences from founders operating in live events  
- Share lessons learned to help other founders plan better  
- Provide insight into budgeting, logistics, and navigating events efficiently  
- Highlight actionable hacks and tips for getting the job done

We are **not looking for polished success stories**. What matters is **learning and honesty**.

---

## How to Contribute

1. **Use the template**: Start with [`template.md`](./template.md) to structure your story.  
2. **Be honest**: Include what went well, what went wrong, and what you learned.  
3. **Optional sections**:  
   - **Budget Snapshot 💰** — no invoices, just numbers  
   - **City Cost-Efficient Tips 🏙️** — practical guidance for operating efficiently in the city you visited 
4. **Submit a Pull Request**: Add your story as a new Markdown file in `/stories` with a descriptive name. Example: `/stories/2025-cardano-summit-buenos-aires.md`


---

## Tips for Writing

- Keep it practical: focus on what other founders can apply.  
- Include context: event, city, year, role, budget situation.  
- Include lessons learned: what you would do differently next time.  
- Optional: share budget numbers and city-specific tips if you feel comfortable.  

---

**Remember:** Your story doesn’t need to be perfect or polished. The goal is to share **real experiences that help other founders survive and thrive in live events**.

# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the spirit of collaboration and shared learning, we as contributors and maintainers of *Founder in the Wild* pledge to:

- Be **respectful** and considerate in our interactions  
- Foster an **inclusive and welcoming** environment for all founders, regardless of background, experience, or geography  
- Focus on **constructive feedback**, learning, and sharing experiences  
- Keep discussions **professional and helpful**, even when disagreeing  

---

## Our Standards

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Be considerate of differing perspectives and experiences  
- Show empathy for contributors and founders sharing their stories  
- Give and receive feedback constructively  
- Assume good intentions in others’ contributions  

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination  
- Abusive, offensive, or derogatory comments  
- Posting content not relevant to the goals of the playbook  
- Any behavior that creates a hostile environment  

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the repository maintainers:

- Email: **sebpabon@gimbalabs.io**
- Or open an issue marked **private** or **sensitive** in the repo

All reports will be **taken seriously and treated confidentially**.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing the Code of Conduct. This may include:

- Removing or editing comments, stories, or contributions that violate the code  
- Issuing warnings to contributors  
- Banning contributors in extreme cases

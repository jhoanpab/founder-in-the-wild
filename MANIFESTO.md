# Manifesto

This playbook was not written from the comfort of a large team,
nor from the budget of a well-funded startup.

It was written from:
- Airports
- Hostels
- Borrowed couches
- Events where we weren’t sure it was worth being there

We believe that:
- If your startup lives only online, it is incomplete
- Entering the physical world early increases the chances of survival
- Money is not the advantage; *courage, intention, and clarity make the difference*.

This is a playbook about *doing more with less*.
And about learning together.
